Section 3: Kalkine’s Unique Mutual Fund Ranking Method

Mutual Funds are scored based on the below set of quantitative criteria’s as per Kalkine’s unique scoring methodology. The objective is to screen the Mutual Funds with a track record of maintaining alpha over benchmark index for one year, maintaining annualized performance for 3 years. alongside lower expense ratio, higher information ratio and sharpe ratio. Only mutual funds that scored highest and met the selection criterias based on all of the below quantitative metrics were further ranked. The scoring was done among all the screened mutual funds based on their performance relative to the mutual fund basket selected.

Kalkine's Mutual Funds Selection Criteria's

Metrics Criteria's Rationale
Fund TNA (AUD million) > AUD 20 mn To identify the funds across the universe of Total Net Asset greater than the AUD 20mn
Alpha for 1 Year to Last Month End (%) Higher the ratio better it is Measures the excess return of the fund over the benchmark return.
Sharpe Ratio for 1 Year to Last Month End (x) Higher the ratio better it is Measures the risk-adjusted return of the fund. This measures the excess return of the fund over the risk-free rate, relative to the standard deviation of the fund's return.
Total Expense Ratio Lower the ratio better it is Measures the total cost involved in running a mutual fund. It is computed as the total expenses of the fund as compared to the fund's return.
Annualized 3 Year Performance to Last Day (%) Higher the ratio better it is Measures the annualized returns fund generated in the past 3 years till the previous trading day
1 Year Performance to Last Day (%) Higher the ratio better it is Funds return over last 365 days
Information Ratio for 1 Year to Last Month End (x) Higher the ratio better it is Measures the consistent active returns generated by the fund manager. At a more granular level, it assesses the returns generated per unit of risk taken.

As a next step, the mutual funds are ranked from low to high, where-in lower the rank, the better it is, considering the better performance versus other mutual funds identified.

Company Name Ranking Methodology
Resolution Capital Real Assets 1 As per Kalkine Methodology for Ranking Mutual Funds, the first step includes identifying a universe of AU-Listed mutual funds under Category of 'Equity Sector Real Estate Australia' as provided by the financial database REFINITIV. The universe identified for this report includes 66 mutual funds. To determine the scoring of each mutual fund within the universe identified, six performance based criteria's are identified which are then used to calculate the cumulative score. For every criteria, a scoring from 1 to 66 is given to every mutual funds based on its value as per criteria's defined. The cumulative score is the addition of individual scores for all the six criteria's. The ranking is decided on the basis of cumulative score. A mutual fund with a higher cumulative score is given a better ranking among the overall universe of 66 mutual funds. The top 6 mutual funds are selected based on the ranking given. However, the score for Resolution Capital Real Assets mutual fund and Pengana High Conviction Property Securities mutual fund is the same, we have further ranked them according to TNA.
